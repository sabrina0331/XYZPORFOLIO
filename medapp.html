MED ENGAGEMENT
Check your medicines and medical records anytime, anywhere

Project Duration: Two Months

Project Type: Team Project

My Team: Carrie Fu(Founder), Po Han Tsheng(Engineer)

My Role: Market Analysts, UI Designer

Background:

Get your medicine records, Lab results, Doctor appointments with one tap.

Carrie is a pharmacist and a teacher who advocates wellness and healthy living. With more than 10 years of pharmacist experience, she handled so many patients by preparing and dispensing prescriptions to them. She always cares about her patients. She wants to build an app that helps users to check their medication and medical record anytime, anywhere.
