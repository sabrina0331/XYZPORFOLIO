About me

I always define myself as a creative person :) I love drawing, handcrafting and often come up with crazy ideas.   I like traveling and hiking. The most interesting place I have been to is Orleans, Louisiana. I love their historical buildings, jazz music everywhere, and people’s lifestyles. I love animals, and I am a cat-mom of a 13-year-old kitty “MiMi”. One of my dreams is to visit Tanzania Safari to see the Great Migration. Global warming is real, I am doing my part to protect the planet, but it’s not enough so that I came up with the idea of the “WasteFree” app, educating people about sorting waste. At work, my colleagues usually called me a responsible and hard-working teammate.
