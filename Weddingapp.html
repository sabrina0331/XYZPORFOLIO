Project Overview

Problem

There are two types of wedding websites right now. It either only has tips and ideas for a specific culture or only has wedding dresses for you to purchase. Is there a website that have both features?

Goal

Help user to find a place that is easy to find wedding ceremony ideas and puchase wedding dresses online.
