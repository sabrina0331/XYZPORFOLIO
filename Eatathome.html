EatatHome

There are nearly 1.3 billion international tourists around the world every year. During their trips, besides going to see all of that famous buildings and learning about local cultures, travellers also enjoy trying local food, but some of those most famous restaurants are always busy and food from other restaurants arenâ€™t authentic at all. How Might We change this situation? That's how I came up with the idea of EatatHome app, which can give you a home eating experience while you are travelling abroad.

Problems

  • It's hard to make a reservation at local famous restaurants beforehand.
  • Food from some restaurants is not authentic at all.
  • Don't feel like fit in local culture, some places local will not eat there.

Solutions

  • Users want a home eating experience while travelling abroad.
  • Meet or talk to local people, making friends with them.
  • Spend reasonable prices to eat authentic food.
