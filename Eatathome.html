EatatHome

There were nearly 1.3 billion international tourists around the world before the pandemic. During their trips, besides going to see all of that famous buildings and learning about local cultures, travellers also enjoy trying local food, but some of those most famous restaurants are always busy and food from other restaurants aren’t authentic at all. Use this app to book a family meal at local families’ homes.

Project Duration

1 Month

Project Type

Conceptual,Google UX Deisgn Course Project

My Role

UX Design, Visual Design, Competitive Audit, User Research, Prototyping, Testing

Problem

The main problem I'd like to solve is to have people book family meal during their trips. There is a potential market for home eating experience. One app has some popularity, but I found out that users who leave bad reviews called it overpriced meal, hosts are professional chef who just wanted to earn money.

Design Process

Empathize

I interviewed more than 10 people about their trip and food hunting experience, I collected those data and created this empathy map.
