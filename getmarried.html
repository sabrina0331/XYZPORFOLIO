Getmarried

An all-in-one application helps people plan their traditional wedding ceremony efficiently.

Getmarried was designed based on a problem by myself. I was planning my Chinese traditional wedding ceremony 2 years ago. I spent about 6 months researching what should I wear and what accessories did I need to buy. I did a lot of paperwork to collect all the information that I need, it frustrated me, but the most exhausting part was finding and trying the traditional wedding dress. There are not many choices in my neighbourhood and the time buying abroad is uncontrollable. I wonder why there wasn't a website/app where I can not only collect those wedding ideas and taboos but also buy traditional dresses and wedding accessories online.
